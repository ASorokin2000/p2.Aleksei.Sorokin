Part C Sketch

Key Assumption about the design

In a real design, it will be a mirror. However, for this project I will try to use webcamera and put the picture from it as a background. It will be instead of the actual mirror

All items are showed in a short version initially. They take around 50% of the screen. The rest works as a mirror.

However, user has two opetions.

1) Tap an item once and open the full version of item. For example, a calendar for the whole day, and not just for the nearest event

2) click quickly 2 times and drag the item. So, it won't take a lot of space. You can use it if you need more space for the actual mirror image.

Features included in the design

1) Time and Date
Located in the bottom right corner. If you double click, it will expand and you can change the time zone and settings.

2) Weather
Located in the bottom right corner, next to the time and date. current temprature and the icon for sunny, windy, rainy weather initially.
If you double click, it will expand and you will see the forecast for the day or for the week.

3) Social Feed
It's connected to your social media profiles and shows how many notificanions, messages etc. you have.
Ideally, You should be able to open the profile in the selected social media if you click on it.
